The Perils of Commuting

This website was originally written when I was pondering moving to a new apartment. I had been reading articles like this saying that long commutes are detrimental to health and happiness. I wanted to find an apartment within 20 minutes of work. However, I was having trouble finding a resource that let me know what neighborhoods fit this requirement. So, this website is the fruit of those frustrations!

I hope you find this website as useful as I have! Please keep in mind while using it that it is in beta mode, and not all locations have been tried. In fact, there are several known bugs that I am actively working on repairing.

Have fun!
